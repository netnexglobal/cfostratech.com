Businesses in India are witnessing the shift of a whole new paradigm, with a tidal wave of technology innovation impacting the finance and business processes and CFOs are at the forefront of this strategic change.

With the central theme of Tech as a Strategy Roadmap for the Finance Function, CFO StraTech Summit and Awards will bring together a community of CFOs who are driven by change and interested in growth to share insights, learn from collective experiences, and explore ways to leverage strategy and technology to achieve sustainable business outcomes.

  May 18, 2023
  8AM Onwards
  Mumbai
The conference will be closed with an awards ceremony, falicatating top CFOs for their exceptional contribution to the fraternity.
