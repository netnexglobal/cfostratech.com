ABOUT

CFO STRATECH 2023

Businesses in India are witnessing the shift of a whole new paradigm, with a tidal wave of technology innovation impacting the finance and business processes and CFOs are at the forefront of this strategic change. As we move from resilience to the building blocks of the future, we'll explore the most essential technologies and revolutionary concepts in business and workforce that are radically changing our future.

CFO StraTech 2023 will bring together a community of CFOs who are driven by change and interested in growth to share insights, learn from collective experiences, and explore ways to leverage strategy and technology to achieve sustainable business outcomes. Experience inspirational keynotes, unrivalled networking opportunities and gain access to the insight you need to be more effective in your role, your team and to drive change within your organisation.

JOIN INDIA'S TOP CFOs IN A DAY OF INSIGHTFUL SESSIONS & INNOVATIVE SOLUTIONS TO INSPIRE YOUR BUSINESS STRATEGY!
